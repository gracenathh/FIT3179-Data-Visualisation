Proportion of Ownership Type and of Store Number in Different Region

Starbucks operates two types of stores: company-owned, licensed, joint venture, and licensed. Currently, the store count is almost equally distributed among these types and even under continuous expansion. During the first quarter of 2021, Starbucks launched 278 new stores. The company's growth is bolstered by a low turnover of its stores. Only 443 Starbucks stores have closed throughout the company's history: 240 stores in 2009, the year of the global financial crisis; 42, in 2010; and 161 in 2011.
